Center of Excellence Scholarship

KPK Government's Initiative for Bright Students

What is Center of Excellence?

The Center of Excellence program is a prestigious initiative by the KPK Government that identifies talented students through the ETEA examination and provides them with world-class education from Class 7th to 12th completely free of cost.

Free Education

Complete education from Class 7th to 12th at government expense

Quality Infrastructure

Study in specially selected schools and colleges with excellent facilities

Bright Future

Gateway to higher education and successful career opportunities
